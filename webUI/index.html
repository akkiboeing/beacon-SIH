About the product

A Handheld or a portable device is created.The device is capable of measuring CO , H, CH4, H2S levels from the atmosphere. The device sends data to a central node outside of the mine or above the surface of the mine via radio communication. Data is then stored in a real time database such as firebase. Data is also made available in real time to the rescuer.The device is also equipped with a gps if necessary. The data received from the device is then analyzed and used to pinpoint the location of the trapped miner and also predict his survival rate over a period of time. An iOS ,android and or web app/windows app is used to create an interface to obtain andanalyze such a data.

Future Goals

The Future goal but an on-going process of this project is to implement Machine Learning algorithms of predictions such as linear or logistic regression that can be used to predict the amount of Fuel , Water and Electricity that may be produced in the future and hence make the product smarter and efficient
